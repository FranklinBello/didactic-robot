Cygnus

Cygnus is the initiative of a group of current and former Miami Dade College students, who want to help those struggling in science classes. By using this website, students that want to receive tutoring in a specific subject will be able to connect with tutors to receive one-on-one tutoring sessions.

The staff of tutors is diverse and in order to offer their services, they had to undergo a filtering process. For current students who want to provide tutoring, they must submit their transcripts and letter of recommendations that prove they excel in the specific subject they want to be mentoring others.
